Privacy Policy for PDFora (Smart PDF Reader)

1. Introduction

Welcome to PDFora (Smart PDF Reader). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring a secure experience while using our mobile application.

PDFora is designed as an offline PDF tool. Viewing, searching, and managing PDF documents occurs entirely on your device without transmitting data to external servers.

By downloading, installing, or using the App, you agree to this Privacy Policy.

2. Our Commitment to "No Data Collection"

PDFora does not collect personal information. The app works without accounts, sign-ups, or personal data sharing. Your documents remain completely private on your device.

3. Information We Do NOT Collect

  • Personal identifiers (name, phone number, email, address)
  • Biometric data
  • Location data
  • Financial information
  • Contact lists
  • Browser history outside the app

4. Permissions and Local Data Processing

4.1 Storage Access

The app requires storage permission only to:

  • Read PDF files stored on your device
  • Maintain a local “Recent Files” list
  • Enable in-document search functionality

Important: All processing happens locally. Files are never uploaded or shared.

4.2 Application Preferences

User settings such as Night Mode are stored locally in the app’s private storage.

5. Technical Data and Logs

  • No server logs are created
  • No IP tracking occurs
  • No internet connection is required for core features

6. Third-Party Services and SDKs

PDFora uses only standard Android and Material Design libraries for interface functionality.

  • No analytics trackers
  • No advertising SDKs
  • No third-party profiling tools

7. Data Retention and Deletion

Since no personal data is collected, there is no server-side storage.

  • All app data remains on your device
  • Clearing app data or uninstalling removes local information
  • No shadow copies of documents are stored

8. Security

  • App operates inside Android sandbox protection
  • No data transmission eliminates interception risks
  • Offline design reduces security exposure

9. Children’s Privacy

PDFora does not collect personal information from any users, including children under 13. The app complies with COPPA and GDPR principles.

10. International Compliance

10.1 GDPR (EEA)

No personal data is transferred or stored. Users control all data directly through their devices.

10.2 CCPA (California)

PDFora does not sell or share personal information.

11. Google Play Data Safety Disclosure

  • Data Collection: None
  • Data Sharing: None
  • Data Transmission: None

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically. Updates will appear here with a revised date.
